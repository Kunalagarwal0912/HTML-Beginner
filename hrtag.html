1 Raj Air Squadron

National Cadet Corps was raised in Rajasthan in 1949 with three units, one each at Jaipur, Udaipur and Jodhpur comprising of 600 Cadets. In the absence of any Circle HQ in Rajasthan, these units were affiliated to HQ 7 Circle Cadet Corps, Delhi. With the expansion of NCC, HQ 14 Circle Cadet Corps was raised at Jaipur in 1954.
